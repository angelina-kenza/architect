    Our Design Process

    At Arcon Innovations we are diligent when it comes to our design & build process.
    From initiation to handover, we remain focused on quality. Working with numerous variables, we do not compromise on delivering our clients requirements. We aim to identify project constraints as early as possible. These tend to include time, budget and scope.
    Our experienced architects work within the set boundaries and aim to maximise what can be done within these parameters.
    For more about the Arcon Innovation services, please kindly fill in the ‘contact us form’ and our team will be in touch.

    Strategic Definition

    By asking the right questions, our consultants, in collaboration with the client, can properly define the project scope. The preparation and briefing process can then begin.

    Concept & Development

    During this stage the initial concept design is produced in line with the requirements of the initial project brief. A number of key tasks need to be progressed during this phase. This includes all cost information, construction and operational strategy. It is recommended that planning applications are submitted during this phase. The final project brief, execution plan and a draft design will be completed.

    Technical Design

    At this stage, we prepare a technical design document to include all architectural, structural and building services information. The level of detail produced by each designer will be dependent on whether the construction onsite will be built in accordance with the information produced by the design team or a specialist subcontractor. By the end of this stage, all aspects of the design will be completed, apart from any potential minor queries arising from the site. These will be concluded onsite.

    Construction

    At this phase of project development, all ideas and designs are materialised.This entails offsite manufacturing and onsite construction. Our experienced architects will be onsite throughout the building construction where they’re required. Site inspections and quality reports will be produced in accordance with the designer’s specific schedule of services.

    Handover & Closeout

    During this phase we facilitate the successful handover of the building in line with the handover strategy agreed in the schedules of services. Various post-project procedures are carried out to make sure the building is safe, secure and meets all quality standards envisioned by the client.

    Interior Design

    Arcon Interiors provide interior design services, this includes consultations through to installation. We unfold potential issues and determine the ideal function and use of the space. The designer is often onsite during "installation" to ensure items are received in good condition, installed correctly and all procedures have been properly carried out.
