    Services

    Arcon Innovations offers an array of services in East Africa.
    We have the resources and capacity to work with global clients as a supplier of bespoke services or on a one-to-one basis.
    As a boutique firm we are flexible and totally dedicated to providing high quality services in all areas.
    Arcon Innovations has developed various styles of architectural buildings.
    From hotels, lodges to contemporary houses.
    Our clients are guaranteed a professional and exciting journey during their project.

    Initial Concept Development

    Every project has a unique concept. The key to getting this project phase right is our teams ability to understand the client’s vision ensuring that we tailor every process to deliver their desired requests.This phase sets the tone for each project.

    Design Development

    After a draft is done and agreed on with the team, we then create an actual design which will be presented to the client. This is one of the most essential stages for every project. Our team of designers are highly skilled and trained in executing each design with precision.

    Detailed Design

    We are extremly thoughtful in our technical approach to design. Arcon architects enjoy taking our clients on an exciting journey and bringing thier ideas to life. We consider the most minute details and seek solutions to potential issues, based on our vast experience.

    Construction (Onsite)

    We pride ourselves on being 100% involved in the development process. We work with selective contractors to make sure the project is coming to life the way the client envisioned it from the start.
